  • SOME HTML TAGS I LEARNED IN WEB DEV COURSE
p
Defines a paragraph, used for structuring and organizing text content.
ul
Creates an unordered list, typically used for presenting a list of items without a specific order.
ol
Generates an ordered list, commonly used for displaying a list of items in a specific sequence or order.
div
Acts as a container or a division in the HTML document, often used for layout and organization of content.
h2
Represents a second-level heading, providing hierarchical structure to the content.
